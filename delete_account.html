Focus Spark – Account Deletion

You can delete your Focus Spark account and all associated data directly from within the app at any time.

⚠️ Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your tasks, quests, journal entries, and other data will be permanently removed.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Focus Spark app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance, you can also request account deletion by contacting us at focusspark.support@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon deletion of your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account credentials (email address)
  • All tasks, habits, categories, and quests
  • Journal entries, notes, and checklists
  • Achievement and progress data

What Data May Be Retained

Anonymous, aggregated analytics data (collected via Firebase Analytics) may be retained as it cannot be linked back to any individual user.
